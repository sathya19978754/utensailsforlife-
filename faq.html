Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping options are available for 1-2 day delivery. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days.

Products

Most of our stainless steel products are dishwasher safe. However, we recommend hand washing for wooden handles and certain specialty items. Check the product description for specific care instructions.

We use only premium materials including 18/10 stainless steel, food-grade silicone, hardwood, and BPA-free plastics. All materials are food-safe and meet the highest quality standards.

Yes! We offer various sets including starter sets, complete cookware sets, and utensil sets. Sets are a great way to save money and ensure all your pieces match perfectly.

Warranty & Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Items must be in new condition. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless defective.

Yes! All our products come with a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects. This covers materials and workmanship but not normal wear and tear or misuse.

Contact our customer service team with your order number and photos of the defective item. We'll review your claim and provide a replacement or repair as needed.
